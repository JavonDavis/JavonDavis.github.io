ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm Javon and I'm a Software Engineer and an AI & Open Source Enthusiast. I enjoy coding, playing basketball and also enjoy sharing my knowledge through teaching and encouraging young Caribbean students to get involved in tech. I've published websites and mobile apps on the Google Play Store and iOS App Store, I've done a little research and presented my work at an international conference, published an online Android Development Course on the Pluralsight Education platform and have done Software Development and QA consulting for a number of years while being a part-time TA at the University of the West Indies(UWI), Mona. Currently I do automation at Automattic and am an Adjunct Lecturer at the UWI.
Top Skills: Mobile & Backend development, Web/Mobile Test Automation, Software & System Design
